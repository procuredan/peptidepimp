AOD-9604

Fat-loss peptide fragment & metabolic support

AOD-9604 (“Advanced Obesity Drug”) is a modified fragment of human growth hormone (HGH), specifically the **176–191 amino acid sequence**, which is believed to play a role in fat metabolism. Unlike HGH, AOD-9604 does not raise IGF-1 levels, which is why many consider it a gentler and more targeted fat-loss peptide.

Commonly Discussed Benefits

  • Enhanced fat burning (especially stubborn belly fat)
  • No increase in blood sugar or IGF-1
  • May improve energy expenditure
  • Often paired with CJC-1295/Ipamorelin for synergy
  • Potential appetite regulation benefits

How It Works

Research suggests AOD-9604 stimulates fat-cell breakdown (lipolysis) while preventing new fat formation (lipogenesis). Many beginners use it during cutting phases or alongside cardio-focused routines for metabolic support.

Common Use Patterns

AOD-9604 is typically used via daily subcutaneous injections, often in the morning or pre-workout. Because it doesn’t affect growth hormone levels, users sometimes run it longer term compared with other peptides.

Safety Notes

Reported side effects are mild and may include temporary redness at the injection site or nausea. As always, discuss any peptide protocol with a licensed medical professional.
