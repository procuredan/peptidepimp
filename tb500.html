TB-500

Recovery & performance peptide guide

TB-500 is a fragment of the naturally occurring protein Thymosin Beta-4. It is widely discussed in the peptide world for its potential to support tissue repair, reduce inflammation, and improve recovery.

Commonly Reported Uses

  • Support for tendon, ligament, and muscle recovery
  • May help improve mobility and reduce stiffness
  • Often combined with BPC-157 for more complete healing support
  • Discussed in the context of performance and injury prevention

How It’s Typically Used

TB-500 is usually taken as a small subcutaneous injection, either systemically (such as in the abdomen) or near the area of concern. Exact dosing and timing varies by protocol and should be guided by a knowledgeable medical provider.

Safety Notes

TB-500 is still considered a research peptide. Most anecdotal reports suggest it is well tolerated, but long-term safety data is limited. Only use peptides under the supervision of a licensed healthcare professional who understands your health history.
