CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin

Growth hormone support & recovery enhancement

CJC-1295 (a growth hormone–releasing hormone analog) and Ipamorelin (a growth hormone secretagogue) are often combined because they synergistically stimulate natural GH and IGF-1 release. This combo is widely discussed among beginners for fat loss, muscle recovery, deeper sleep, and anti-aging purposes.

Commonly Reported Benefits

  • Improved fat metabolism (especially belly fat)
  • Enhanced muscle recovery and performance
  • Better, deeper sleep and vivid dreams
  • Anti-aging benefits via boosted natural GH output
  • Increased collagen production for skin & joints

How It’s Typically Used

Most protocols include daily or 5-day-per-week subcutaneous injections, often in the evening to match natural GH release. CJC-1295 is usually used in its **no-DAC** form when paired with Ipamorelin for a shorter, pulse-based release pattern.

Safety Notes

Reported side effects may include temporary hunger, light flushing, tingling, or water retention. People with underlying medical conditions or cancer history should consult a specialist before using any GH-related peptide. Always follow medical guidance.
