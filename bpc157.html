BPC-157

Healing & gut health peptide guide

BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound-157) is a peptide widely discussed for its potential benefits in tendon and ligament repair, gut health, inflammation reduction, and accelerated recovery.

Commonly Reported Uses

  • Supports tendon and ligament healing
  • May improve joint mobility and reduce inflammation
  • Frequently discussed for gut issues (ulcers, IBS, leaky gut)
  • Used for soft-tissue injuries and overuse recovery

How It’s Typically Used

BPC-157 is commonly taken as a small subcutaneous injection near the injured area or the abdomen. Dosing varies by provider, and medical guidance is always recommended.

Safety Notes

Research is ongoing. Most users report it is well tolerated, but side effects and responses vary. Always consult a licensed medical professional before starting any peptide.
